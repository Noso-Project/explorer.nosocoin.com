Noso Governance

What is NOSO GVT?

Built into Noso protocol is a Governance Token (GVT) poll management system. This system allows GVT owners to create project related polls for consideration which are voted upon by other GVT owners. In order to participate in the voting process, an individual must acquire through purchase or trade, a Noso Governance Token (GVT). These tokens are available to anyone wanting to participate in the Noso voting process.

Governance Tokens have the following specifications:
  • GVTs are transferable
  • 1 GVT = 1 vote
  • Any GVT owner can start a poll
  • Polls with +75% of approbation will be mandatory; +50% will be recommended
  • New GVTs can be minted every year only (on demand, after government poll approbation)
  • GVTs can be banned by other GVTs owners in certain circumstances. When banned, the GVT owner becomes suspended from creating new polls or participating on existing ones for 90 days.
  • There can be only one GVT poll at a time
  • GVTs rules can be adjusted by a GVT votation
